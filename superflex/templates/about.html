About DynastySuperFlex

Helping fantasy football managers make better decisions since 2021.

Our Mission

DynastySuperFlex is the ultimate resource for fantasy football managers. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, data-driven insights that help you make informed decisions about your fantasy teams. By combining data from multiple trusted sources with your league information, we offer a unique perspective that can give you the edge in your fantasy leagues.

What We Offer

Our platform integrates with your Sleeper leagues to deliver:

  • Dynasty power rankings based on multiple data sources
  • Contender rankings to assess your team's competitive position
  • Trade analysis tools
  • Player value assessments
  • League analytics and insights

Our Data Sources

We aggregate data from several trusted fantasy football sources including:

  • KeepTradeCut
  • FantasyPros
  • FantasyCalc
  • Dynasty Process
  • ESPN Projections
  • NFL Projections

Moving Forward

DynastySuperFlex is moving to a new platform at FantasyNavigator.com. The new site includes exciting features like an enhanced trade calculator and detailed league analytics.
